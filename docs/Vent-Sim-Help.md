# ICU Ventilator Simulator: Help & About
**Interactive Respiratory Mechanics Simulation**

### Purpose
A high-fidelity visual aid for understanding the interplay between patient physiology and mechanical support.

### How to Use
1. **Patient Setup:** Adjust Resistance (Raw) and Compliance (Crs) sliders.
2. **Modes:** Supports VCV, PCV, PSV+PEEP, and APRV.
3. **Advanced Interaction:** Click **"Ins. Hold"** to measure Plateau Pressure or **"Exp. Hold"** to assess for Auto-PEEP.



### Technical Note
Designed for **PC/Desktop browsers**. It will not run correctly on mobile phones or small-screen devices due to high-resolution waveform rendering.
