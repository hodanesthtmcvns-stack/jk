# NEWS2 Triage App: Help & About

### Purpose
Standardizes the assessment of acute illness severity in adults using the **National Early Warning Score (NEWS2)** framework. It identifies patients at risk of clinical deterioration.

### How to Use
1. **Input Parameters:** Enter Respiratory Rate, SpO2 (Scale 1 or 2), Air/Oxygen, Systolic BP, Heart Rate, Consciousness (ACVPU), and Temperature.
2. **Calculate:** The tool automatically generates the total score.
3. **Review Response:** Follow the color-coded clinical response triggers (Low, Medium, or High risk).



### Critical Safety
* **Red Flag:** A single score of **3** in any parameter or a total of **7** requires immediate emergency assessment.
* **Exclusions:** Not for use in children (<16 years) or pregnant women.
* **Privacy:** 100% offline; no data leaves your device.
